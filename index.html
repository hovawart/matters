The process

From reaching out to your first session

Three steps, usually under a week. No forms longer than a page, no waitlist you can't see the end of.

01

Tell us what you're looking for

A short, private form — what's going on, what you'd find helpful, and when you're generally free. Takes about five minutes.

02

We match you with a supervised volunteer

A student counselor is paired to your situation by our clinical team, with a licensed mentor assigned to oversee the case from day one.

03

Begin free, regular sessions

Meet weekly or biweekly, in person or by video. Your volunteer debriefs every session with their mentor to keep your care on track.

Why it's safe

Every case has a licensed mentor attached to it

Volunteers aren't left to figure it out alone. Here's what oversight actually looks like, case by case. Read more about our approach.

Mentors are licensed clinicians LCSWs and licensed psychologists review every case before it opens and stay attached to it throughout.
Weekly session debriefs, mandatory Volunteers walk their mentor through each session — what came up, what they said, what they'd do differently.
A mentor is always reachable If something urgent comes up mid-session, volunteers have a direct line to their mentor — same day, no exceptions.
Confidentiality, with clear limits stated upfront Standard clinical confidentiality applies, and the few legal exceptions (like risk of harm) are explained before your first session.
