Volunteer with us

Apply to counsel, under real supervision.

Open to current graduate students in social work, clinical psychology, or a closely related program. Takes about ten minutes to apply.

What happens next

  1. Our volunteer coordinators review your application, usually within two weeks.
  2. If it's a fit, we schedule a short intake interview and pair you with a mentor.
  3. You complete a brief onboarding before your first supervised case.

We keep caseloads small — most volunteers carry 2–4 clients at a time, alongside weekly supervision.
